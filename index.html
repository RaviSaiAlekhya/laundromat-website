About Washy Washy World

Your trusted laundry service in New Bedford

Our Story

Washy Washy World was founded with a simple mission: to provide the New Bedford community with clean, affordable, and convenient laundry services. We understand that laundry is a chore that everyone has to do, but nobody really enjoys.

That's why we've created a welcoming environment with state-of-the-art equipment to make your laundry experience as pleasant and efficient as possible. Our friendly staff is always available to assist you, and we offer multiple service options to fit your busy lifestyle.

Whether you prefer to do your own laundry using our self-service machines, drop it off for our expert team to handle, or have us pick it up and deliver it to your doorstep, Washy Washy World is here to serve you.
